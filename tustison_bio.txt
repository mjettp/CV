Nicholas J. Tustison was educated at Brigham Young University,
the University of Virginia, Washington University in St. Louis, and
the University of Pennsylvania.  He is currently in the radiology department
at the University of Virginia.  His research interests include the
development of robust open source software.  Notable contributions
include image registration, segmentation, bias correction, and other
image analysis algorithms available as open source in the ANTS toolkit
(http://www.picsl.upenn.edu/ANTS) and the Insight Toolkit
(http://www.itk.org).
